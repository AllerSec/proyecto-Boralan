Privacy policy

Boralan complies with the current personal data protection regulations: Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 of 5 December on the Protection of Personal Data and Guarantee of Digital Rights (LOPDGDD).

1. Data controller

  • Controller: Boralan
  • Address: Lesaka, Navarre (Spain)
  • Email: boralan04@gmail.com
  • Phone: 628 850 027

2. Purpose of processing

The data you provide through the contact form (name, phone, email, locality and message) is processed solely to respond to your request, prepare a quote and maintain commercial contact related to our pruning and felling services.

3. Legal basis

The legal basis for processing your data is your consent, given when you submit the form, and the legitimate interest in responding to your enquiry.

4. Data retention

We will keep your data for as long as necessary to handle your request and, thereafter, for the legally required periods. When it is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted.

5. Recipients

We do not share your data with third parties, except where legally required. Form submission may be handled through a form-services provider acting as a data processor in accordance with the GDPR. Your email client may be used as an alternative submission method.

6. Your rights

You may exercise your rights of access, rectification, erasure, objection, restriction of processing and portability at any time by writing to us at boralan04@gmail.com. If you consider that the processing does not comply with the regulations, you may lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (aepd.es).

7. Security

We apply the technical and organisational measures necessary to ensure the security of your data and to prevent its alteration, loss or unauthorised access.
