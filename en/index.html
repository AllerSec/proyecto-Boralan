What we do

Pruning and felling of dangerous trees, in impossible spots.

Boralan carries out controlled felling, pruning at height, work in inaccessible areas and management of pruning and felling waste in Navarre and the Basque Country, using climbing techniques and controlled dismantling.

People call us when a tree poses a risk or when machinery can't get in. We climb, cut it piece by piece and lower it under control.
