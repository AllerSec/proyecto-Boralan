What we do
at height.

Climbing, controlled dismantling and clean-up. We tackle the trees no one else will touch.

Service 01

Controlled felling

We fell large trees piece by piece from the top, without dropping them all at once. This protects nearby houses, fences, power lines and installations. Every cut is controlled and lowered by rope.

  • Piece-by-piece felling

    No risk to the surroundings.

  • Dangerous trees

    Specimens at risk of falling or already split.

Service 02

Pruning at height

We climb into the canopy with climbing techniques to prune precisely: dead, dangerous or in-the-way branches. We improve the tree's health and reduce risk without harming it.

  • Climbing techniques

    Access to canopies machinery can't reach.

  • Maintenance pruning

    The tree's health, shape and safety.

Service 03

Inaccessible areas

We work where machinery can't reach: slopes, enclosed gardens, beside buildings or on impossible terrain. With no cranes or heavy gear needed, we lower the cost of the job.

  • No heavy machinery

    Lower cost and less damage to the surroundings.

  • Difficult terrain

    Slopes, fenced areas and tight spaces.

Service 04

Waste management

We don't leave the job half done. We handle the pruning and felling debris and leave the area clean and tidy.

  • Debris removal

    Collection and management of wood and branches.

  • Clean area

    We finish and leave everything tidy.

How we work

From the call to the tree on the ground.

A simple, safe process — always all three of us on the job.

Step 01

We assess the tree

We look at the height, the surroundings and the difficulty. Every tree is different.

Step 02

We climb and cut

We climb, tie in and cut in a controlled way, piece by piece.

Step 03

We clean the area

We manage the waste and leave everything tidy.

Frequently asked questions

Common questions about pruning and felling at height.

How much does it cost to fell a large tree at height?

The cost depends on the height, the surroundings and how hard the access is. By working with climbing and no crane, we lower the price compared with heavy machinery. We give a free, no-obligation quote.

When do I need a controlled felling at height?

When a tree is at risk of falling, is close to a house or fence, or machinery cannot get in. We climb the tree and cut it piece by piece in a controlled way.

Can you remove a tree right next to a house without damaging it?

Yes. We dismantle the tree piece by piece from above and lower each cut with a rope, protecting nearby houses, fences and installations.

Do you work where machinery cannot get in?

Yes. We specialise in areas inaccessible to cranes and machines. By working with climbing techniques we lower costs because we don't need heavy equipment.

Do you take care of removing the pruning or felling debris?

Yes, we manage the pruning and felling waste and leave the area clean.

Which areas do you work in?

In Navarre (Lesaka, Bera, Igantzi, Etxalar and the rest of Bortziri / Cinco Villas) and in Gipuzkoa (Irun, Hondarribia and the surrounding area). Tell us about your case with no obligation.

Got a tree to sort out?

Tell us about it and we'll explain how we'd do it.
