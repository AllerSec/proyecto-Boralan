Cookie policy

This website uses cookies to improve the browsing experience. Below we explain what they are and how we use them.

1. What are cookies?

A cookie is a small file stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow preferences to be remembered and information about browsing to be obtained.

2. Cookies we use

  • Technical and preference cookies (own): we use your browser's local storage to remember whether you have accepted the cookie notice and to show the loading effect only on your first visit. They do not collect identifiable personal data.
  • Third-party cookies: when playing embedded content or following links to Instagram, Facebook or map services, those third parties may install their own cookies, subject to their respective policies.

3. Managing cookies

You can accept or decline cookies through the notice that appears when you enter the site. You can also set your browser to block or delete cookies at any time:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

4. Updates

We may update this policy to adapt it to regulatory or technical changes. We recommend reviewing it periodically.
